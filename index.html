Welcome to Dotfiles’s documentation!

Welcome to my personal dotfiles repository! Here, you’ll find my collection of configuration files for various tools and applications that I use on a daily basis. These dotfiles are tailored to streamline my development environment and enhance productivity across Unix-based systems.

Overview

Dotfiles are used to customize your system’s environment. This repository serves as a central hub to store, manage, and share my configurations for applications such as the shell (bash), editors (Vim/Neovim), and many others. Whether you’re looking to adopt some of my configurations or just curious about different ways to optimize your development environment, feel free to explore and borrow whatever you find useful.
